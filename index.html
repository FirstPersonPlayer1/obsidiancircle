Obsidian Circle is a private research publication focused on the observation of capital movement, market structure, and historical financial behavior. We publish analytical commentary for informational purposes only and do not provide investment advice, recommendations, or solicitations.

What We Publish

  • Observed capital movement across markets and structures
  • Structural market behavior at a macro level
  • Historical financial patterns and precedents
  • Institutional behavior in aggregated form

What We Do Not Do

  • Provide investment or financial advice
  • Make recommendations or endorsements
  • Offer personalized guidance
  • Solicit or participate in transactions

Access to Obsidian Circle is limited and added periodically. Interested parties may request to be notified when access becomes available.

Obsidian Circle publishes observational research and analytical commentary for informational purposes only. Nothing on this site constitutes investment advice, financial advice, a recommendation, or a solicitation of any kind. Obsidian Circle does not assess individual circumstances or suitability.
